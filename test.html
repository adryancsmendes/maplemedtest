Pathologist

The Pathologist is responsible for diagnosing diseases by examining tissues, cells, and organs.

Radiologist

The Radiologist uses imaging techniques to diagnose and treat diseases.

Nuclear Medicine Radiologist

The Nuclear Medicine Radiologist specializes in diagnosing diseases using radioactive materials.

Radiation Oncologist

The Radiation Oncologist treats cancer and other diseases using radiation therapy.

Surgeon

The Surgeon performs operations to treat or prevent diseases, repair injuries, or improve bodily functions.

Respirologist

The Respirologist diagnoses and treats diseases of the lungs and respiratory system.
